About Us

Angelic Treasures Child Care is where quality child care and education meets family values. From French offerings to age-appropriate activities, we offer an array of programs focused on your child’s social, cognitive, emotional, psychological, and physical development. Plus, we provide an unparalleled home-away-from-home environment to ensure your child has an enjoyable and comfortable learning experience.

We love your angels as much as you do, so it’s no wonder we’re the best at what we do.
